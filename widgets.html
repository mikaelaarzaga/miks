About

HYASENSE

It is an automated water hyacinth collector where machine learning was integrated to be the decision maker of the system. It has a camera that captures the current status of the plants where the device is located, it will then be analyzed by the machine learning algorithm, which is the SVM, via the device's mini-computer, the Raspberry Pi. There are 3 levels of infestation and these are the: LOW-LEVEL, MODERATE-LEVEL and HIGH-LEVEL. When the level is at moderate and high, that is the only time that the device will work and start collecting water hyacinth. After the Machine Learning classify the current infestation status, it will then send a signal to the two arduino in the device, if the motor and water pump will turn on or not.

This project aims to help the fishermen on sailing smoothly from the shore to the lake. The Hyasense is a remote monitoring device, it already has its own power supply system. Anyone can check the latest status of the water hyacinth infestation via this web application. The collected hyacinths by the deice must also be brought out of the device for it to keep collecting.

The proponents of the Hyasense hopes that the device will lessen the negative imapcts of the Water Hyacinth in the lake both in environmental aspect and for the community around it.
